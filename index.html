    How to use
    1. Pick what you want from the chips up top — Food, Coffee, Gas, EV (tap more than one).
    2. Tap Start driving to use your live GPS. Results refresh as you drive.
    3. Set a Destination (or tap Home / a ⭐ favorite) to aim suggestions that way — or leave it on 📍 Live to follow your heading.
    4. Use Best / Closest / Cheapest to re-sort, and Open now to hide closed spots.
    5. Tap the blue arrow for turn-by-turn in Maps. Tap a row to find it on the 🗺 map (and a pin to find its row).
    About

    Hino’s RoadBite finds well-rated restaurants, coffee, gas, and EV charging just ahead of you on a drive — ranked by how little they add to your route. Built for the Philadelphia ⇄ Lewiston, ME run. Place data and ratings from Google.
